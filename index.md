# Privacy Policy – Wevent

**Last updated:** 2026-03-01

Wevent (“Wevent”, “we”, “our”, “us”) provides a mobile application that allows users to discover, request quotes, book and pay for event-related services (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the App.

## 1) Who this policy applies to
This policy applies to:
- **Clients** who browse services, request quotes, book services, and make payments.
- **Providers** (service professionals) who list services, communicate with clients, send offers/quotes, and fulfill bookings.

## 2) Information we collect

### A. Account and profile information
- Name (or business name), email address, phone number (optional), profile details.
- Account type (Client/Provider).
- Provider details (e.g., service category, service description, pricing, availability, and portfolio photos if you upload them).

### B. Booking and transaction information
- Service requests and event details you provide (e.g., event date, city/location, type of event, special instructions).
- Quotes/offers, booking status, and cancellations.
- Payment status and payment-related metadata (e.g., transaction ID, payment confirmation).

### C. Messages and user content
- Chat messages between clients and providers.
- Files you upload (e.g., images, attachments) if the feature exists.
- Reviews/ratings and support requests.

### D. Device and usage information
- Device information (model, operating system version), app version, and language.
- Logs and diagnostic data (crash reports, performance data) to improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

### E. Location (optional)
If you enable location features, we may process approximate location (e.g., city/area) to help match services near you. You can disable location permissions in your device settings.

## 3) How we use your information
We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account.
- Provide core features: browsing services, requesting quotes, booking, payments, chat, and notifications.
- Match clients and providers and facilitate communication.
- Improve security (fraud prevention, abuse detection) and maintain the App.
- Provide customer support and respond to requests.
- Send service notifications (booking updates, payment confirmations, important service messages).

## 4) Payments (Stripe)
Payments in the App are processed by **Stripe**. When you make a payment, your payment information (such as card details) is collected and processed by Stripe according to their policies.

**Wevent does not store full card numbers or full payment card details.** Wevent may store:
- payment status (paid/unpaid),
- transaction identifiers (e.g., Stripe payment ID),
- and receipts or invoice references (if applicable).

## 5) Sharing of information
We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share information in the following situations:

### A. Between clients and providers
To enable bookings and service fulfillment, we share relevant information between the client and the provider, such as:
- profile name and basic contact details (as needed for the booking),
- service request details,
- messages and booking/scheduling information.

### B. Service providers (processors)
We use third-party service providers to operate the App (e.g., hosting, databases, analytics, crash reporting, push notifications, and **Stripe** for payments). These providers process data on our behalf to provide their services.

### C. Legal obligations
We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and prevent fraud.

## 6) Data retention
We retain personal data only as long as necessary to:
- provide the App and manage bookings,
- comply with legal obligations,
- resolve disputes and enforce our terms.

You may request deletion of your account (see “Your rights”).

## 7) Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8) Your rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- access, correct, or delete your personal information,
- withdraw consent (where applicable),
- request a copy of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us.

## 9) Children’s privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us so we can delete it.

## 10) Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will post updates on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
